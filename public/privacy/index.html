Privacy

Last updated: May 2026

The short version

We collect as little as possible. We use your email only to deliver what you asked for and a brief follow-up series. We never sell, rent, or share your email. You can unsubscribe any time.

What we collect

  • Email address — when you request cheat sheets or order a guide.
  • Your selections — which cheat-sheet volumes or cases you requested, and optional details you provide (school, course, patient alias) to customize a guide.
  • Basic delivery metadata — confirmation status and timestamps, so we can deliver reliably and prevent duplicate sends.

How we use it

  • To deliver the resources or guides you requested.
  • To send a short clinical-insight follow-up series (you can opt out at any time).
  • To respond to support messages you send us.

What we don't do

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not use your data for third-party advertising. We keep subscriber data only as long as needed and minimize what we store.

Email & consent

We use double opt-in: after you submit your email, you confirm via a link before we send anything. Every email includes a one-click unsubscribe, which we always honor.

Your choices

  • Unsubscribe from any email using the link in the footer.
  • Request deletion of your data by emailing us.

Contact

Privacy questions or deletion requests? Email support@clinicalperformancelab.com.
