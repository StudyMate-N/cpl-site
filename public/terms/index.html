1. What CPL is

We provide a free practice simulator, free cheat-sheet PDFs, and paid case guides. CPL is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to iHuman, any school, or any institution.

Our materials are study aids. They are intended to help you understand and practice clinical reasoning — not to be submitted as your own original work where that would violate your program's policies. Nothing on CPL is medical advice. Medication references and clinical content reflect what specific iHuman case templates expect and may not be safe or appropriate for real patients. Always verify dosing and clinical decisions with an authoritative source before any clinical application.
