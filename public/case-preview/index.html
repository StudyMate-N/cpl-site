The traps that quietly cost points

These are the point-loss patterns iHuman never tells you about. We map every one for this case.

Order zero tests. Exam is normal — this is a clinical diagnosis. Ordering anything is penalized.
⚠
Ordering CT or MRI triggers a "harmful to patient" flag → your Tests score drops to 0%.
